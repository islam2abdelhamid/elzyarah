About Us

Elzyrah application was launched in 2020 as an innovation aiming directly for the pharma industry by introducing cutting edge mobile technologies into new business areas for accelerating the business cycles, increase productivity, reduce operating costs and extend the value chain infrastructure through enterprise mobile business solutions, also Elzyzrah has successfully endeavored into various verticals including Surveys, Sales, Marketing, Inspections and much more.

Elzyarah is considered integrated applications that complete the loop of healthcare value chain starting pharmaceutical manufacture till the pharmacist passing through all HCPs, Elzyarah is merely the first step in a long future of technological innovations that will pave the way to healthcare sector in order to make it more intelligent, trusted and safe seeking for improve the human quality of life.

Elzyarah is also a consolidated tool where contacts, chatting, communication, schedules, videos, info-graphs, call data and spreadsheets updates can be synched to a simple platform. This information can then be shared and accessed on various devices.
